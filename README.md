## 📌 Overview

This project performs **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** on the Global Terrorism Dataset to uncover hidden patterns, trends, and insights related to terrorist activities across the globe.

It focuses on **data-driven understanding** of attacks, regions, targets, and their impact.

---
## 📂 Dataset
- Source: Global Terrorism Database (GTD)  
- Time Period: 1970–2017  
- Records: 180,000+ incidents  
- Features: 100+ attributes (location, attack type, target, casualties, etc.)

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

- 🔍 Identify high-risk regions and countries  
- 📊 Analyze attack patterns and frequency  
- 🎯 Study target types and weapon usage  
- 📉 Understand impact based on casualties

## 📈 Advanced Insights 🚀

- ⚡ **Power Law Pattern**  
  Majority of attacks are low impact, while a few cause massive casualties  

- 🌍 **Regional Specialization**  
  Different regions prefer different attack methods  

- 📊 **Temporal Escalation**  
  Significant rise in attacks in the modern era  

- 🎯 **Target Evolution**  
  Increasing focus on civilian targets over time
